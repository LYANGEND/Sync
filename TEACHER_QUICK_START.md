### 1. Start Your Day at the Dashboard
- Always begin at the Teacher Dashboard (/)
- Review your schedule and pending tasks

## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Problem: I can't see my classes
**Solution**: 
1. Check if you're assigned to classes (contact admin)
2. Verify you're logged in as a TEACHER role
3. Clear browser cache and log in again

### Problem: I can't create homework
**Solution**:
1. Ensure you're in Teacher > Homework (not AI Teacher)
2. Check if you have classes assigned
3. Verify you have TEACHER role permissions

### Problem: Students can't see my homework
**Solution**:
1. Check if homework is published (not draft)
2. Verify correct class is selected
3. Check due date is in the future

### Problem: I'm always ending up in AI Teacher
**Solution**:
1. Bookmark the Dashboard page: `/`
2. Click "Dashboard" in sidebar instead of "AI Teacher"
3. Set your browser homepage to the dashboard
