## Key Insights

### Why Generic LMS Fails in Schools

1. **Too Complex**
   - Teachers don't have time to build courses
   - Students just want homework
   - Parents can't navigate

2. **Wrong Focus**
   - Optimized for self-paced learning
   - Schools need classroom support
   - Exams, not certificates

3. **Technology Barriers**
   - Requires good internet
   - Needs powerful devices
   - Expensive to run

4. **Cultural Mismatch**
   - Designed for Western markets
   - Doesn't fit African context
   - Ignores local constraints

### Why School-Focused LMS Works

1. **Simple**
   - Post homework in 30 seconds
   - Check grades instantly
   - No training needed

2. **Right Focus**
   - Supports classroom teaching
   - Aligned with curriculum
   - Exam preparation

3. **Technology Fit**
   - Works offline
   - Runs on any device
   - Affordable

4. **Cultural Fit**
   - SMS for parents
   - WhatsApp integration
   - Print-friendly
   - Local support
