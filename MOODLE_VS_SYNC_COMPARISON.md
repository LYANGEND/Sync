## üéì Context: Why Our Approach Differs

### Moodle's Context
- Universities and colleges
- High-speed internet
- Individual student accounts
- Self-paced learning
- Global audience

### Sync's Context
- Primary and secondary schools in Zambia
- Limited internet access
- Shared parent accounts
- Teacher-led instruction
- Exam-focused (ECZ)
- 40+ students per class
- Shared devices

### Our Design Philosophy
1. **Speed First**: 30 seconds to post homework
2. **Bulk Operations**: Grade 40 students quickly
3. **Offline Capable**: Work without internet
4. **Mobile-First**: Works on any phone
5. **Print-Friendly**: Can be printed for offline use
6. **SMS Integration**: Reach parents without internet
7. **Shared Accounts**: Parents manage multiple children
8. **All-in-One**: School management + LMS
